Classes are camel case starting with a captical letter. The fact that they are a class along with their domain is stated before their class name and seprated from the class name with an underscore: 

	UIClass_ClassName | UIClass_ClassyClass | UIClass_HerpaDerpClass
	GameObjectClass_Class | GameObjectClass_ClassyClass | GameObjectClass_HerpaDerpClass
	GameMathClass_Class | GameMathClass_ClassyClass | GameMathClass_HerpaDerpClass


Scripts are formated the same as Classes, excepr their descriptor starts lowercase:

	uiScript_ScriptName | uiScript_ClassyScript | uiScript_HerpaDerpScript
	gameObjectScript_Script | gameObjectScript_ClassyScript | gameObjectScript_HerpaDerpScript
	gameMathScript_Script | gameMathScript_ClassyScript | gameMathScript_HerpaDerpScript


Funtions/methods are spaced by underscores, the return type is at the start of the name and followed by its subtype of eithr method (mthd) or normal function (func); this sirname is in camel case starting with a lower case letter. The rest of the name is lowercase with underscores used for spaces:

	funcVoid_func | funcVoid_func_y | funcVoid_func_y_time
	funcInt_func | funcInt_func_y | funcInt_func_y_time
	funcString_func | funcString_func_y | funcString_func_y_time
	
	mthdVoid_method | mthdVoid_method_y | mthdVoid_method_y_time
	mthdInt_method | mthdInt_method_y | mthdInt_method_y_time
	mthdString_method | mthdString_method_y | mthdString_method_y_time


Vars are lowercase-starting camel case with the first word always being the type:

	intVar | intVaryVar | intTheMostVar
	stringVar | stringVaryVar | stringTheMostVar
	floatVar | floatVaryVar | floatTheMostVar


External files are all lowercase with spaces replaced by underscores, and type of file as the the first word:

	folder_name | folder_name_is | folder_name_is_cool
	image_name | image_name_is | image_name_is_cool
	txt_name | txt_name_is | txt_name_is_cool
